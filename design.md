# Cuvatex – Design Specification Document

## Source Reference

Design inspired from provided motion video prototype.

---

## 1. Design Philosophy

**Style:** Minimal, premium, futuristic, clean
**Mood:** Professional, confident, technical
**Brand Feel:** High-end digital agency, product-focused

Principles:

* Clarity over decoration
* Space over density
* Structure over chaos
* Precision over complexity

---

## 2. Visual Language

### Color System

* Primary: #09524C
* Black: #0B0B0B
* Dark Gray: #1A1A1A
* Light Gray: #F4F6F7
* White: #FFFFFF

Usage Rules:

* Primary color only for accents, CTAs, highlights
* Never saturate full sections with primary color
* Dark backgrounds for premium sections

---

### Typography

**Primary Font:** Poppins

Hierarchy:

* H1: 56–64px, SemiBold
* H2: 40–48px, SemiBold
* H3: 28–32px, Medium
* Body: 16–18px, Regular
* Small text: 13–14px

Rules:

* Strong vertical rhythm
* Clear hierarchy
* No decorative fonts

---

## 3. Layout System

### Grid

* Desktop: 12-column grid
* Tablet: 8-column grid
* Mobile: 4-column grid

Margins:

* Desktop: 120px
* Tablet: 64px
* Mobile: 24px

---

## 4. Motion & Interaction

### Animations

* Fade-in
* Slide-up
* Micro-transitions

Rules:

* No heavy animations
* No parallax overload
* Motion only for hierarchy and flow

---

## 12. Final Design Rules

Do:

* Use space
* Use structure
* Use hierarchy

Don’t:

* Over-animate
* Over-decorate
* Over-color
* Over-text

**Design Identity Summary:**
Cuvatex must feel like a premium engineering brand, not a marketing agency.
It should communicate competence, precision, and trust — visually and structurally.
